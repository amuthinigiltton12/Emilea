Fitness Routine

Let us see how I take care of my health by keeping a fitness routine!

  • Warm-up (4 minutes)
  • March or jog in place - 1 min
  • Arm circles (forward and backward) - 30 sec each
  • Bodyweight squats - 1 min
  • Hip circles and leg swings - 1 min
  • Main Workout (12-15 minutes)
  • Push-ups - 10-15 reps
  • Bodyweight squats - 15-20 reps
  • Lunges - 10 reps per leg
  • Plank - 30-60 seconds hold
  • Glute bridges - 12-15 reps
  • Cardio Finisher (5-8 minutes)
  • Jumping jacks - 1 minute
  • High knees - 1 minute
  • Mountain climbers – 1 minute
  • Repeat circuit 2 times
  • Core Focus (Optional – 5 minutes)
  • Crunches – 15 reps
  • Bicycle crunches – 20 reps
  • Leg raises – 12–15 reps
  • Side plank – 20–30 seconds each side
  • Cool Down (5–8 minutes)
  • Hamstring stretch – 30 seconds per leg
  • Quad stretch – 30 seconds per leg
  • Shoulder stretch – 30 seconds per side
  • Deep breathing – 1–2 minutes

Quick Glance:Be hydrated and include a balanced diet on your plate for better results.
